Setting up is really easy

Simply follow the instructions in the video below. In short we're allowing for remote devices to start your Sony BRAVIA Android TV, and set-up an authentication through a pre-shared key.

All settings that need adjustment are under the Network properties of your TV. From these network settings you need to set:

  • Remote start: Enabled
  • Home network setup
    • Authentication: Normal and Pre-shared Key
    • Pre-shared key: Anything, but remember it

After this you are ready to continue setting up your Sony BRAVIA Android TV. You will be prompted for your pre-shared key at a later point.
